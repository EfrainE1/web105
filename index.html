RED DEAD REDEMPTION 2

A game created by Rockstar Games

Summary of the game

Red Dead Redemption 2 starts with the Dutch van Der Linde gang desperate to find shelter while in a blizzard. After a botched bank robbery in Blackwater, they are forced to flee into the mountains. Some of the members are either dead or missing. Arthur Morgan, the main character, is sent to find fellow gang member, John Marston. Once Arthur finds him, they run into their rival gang, the O'Driscolls and kill some of their members. After a succesful train robbery, the gang settle by a town called Valentine.

After another failed robbery in Valetine, which causes the gang to move to a town called Rhodes. Rhodes is a town who is primarily run by two wealthy familes, the Grays and the Braithwaites. After a rumor spreads to the gang that both of the families have large amounts of gold, the gang tries to locate the gold. Turns out, the families learned of their intentions to steal the gold and ambush the gang. During the ambush, John's son, Jack is kidnapped by the Braithwaite family. They learn that Jack is in a much bigger town called Saint Denis, which starts their next adventure.

The gang later learns that Jack is with a major crime lord named Angelo Bronte. Dutch Van der Linde and the gang are able to negotiate with Bronte and Jack is released. After an attack by their rival gang, Dutch starts to become more reckless with the gang. Dutch later learns that Bronte tricked them about a potential heist, and the gang kill him. Dutch decides to rob the Saint Denis bank, which is heavily guarded. Two prominent members of the gang are killed and John is captured by the police. Some of the members end up on a boat and the boat sinks during a storm. The members somehow survive and end up on an island called Guarma. Arthur and the gang are able to get back to Saint Denis, but sadly, Arthur is diagnosed with Tuberculosis and is slowly dying.

John breaks out of jail with the help of Arthur, but there is a major rift in the gang. Arthur finally sees Dutch for he is, and he flees with John. At this point of the game, you have two choices, you can help John escape with his family or you can go back to locate large sums of money that Dutch was holding onto. During this time, Arthur tells John "Be loyal to what matters." - Arthur Morgan, Red Dead Redemption 2 Both end in the same result, Arthur fights with another member called Micah, who was secretly working with the federal government. Due to Arthur's sickness, he is extremely weak and dies. Micah survives along with Dutch. This doesn't end the game though, you can continue the story with John. John struggles to live life without the gang, and in order to survive he must face society.
